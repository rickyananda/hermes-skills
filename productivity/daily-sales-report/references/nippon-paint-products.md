# Nippon Paint Product Categories & Naming

## Product Categories

### CCM (Colour Creations Mix) / Tinting
- Products that require color mixing at the store
- Categories: Pastel, Tint, Deep, Accent (price varies by color depth)
- Key products: Weatherbond series, Spot-less, Easywash, Vinilex

### RM (Ready Mix)
- Pre-mixed products ready to sell
- No tinting required
- Examples: Bee Brand, Elastex, Woodstain, Komilex, Pylox

### Produk Fokus
- Products with specific sales targets
- Usually require minimum 1 gallon purchase
- Examples: Weatherbond 1001, Spot-less 1001

## Common Product Names (Indonesian Market)

### Cat Tembok (Wall Paint)
- **Weatherbond** - Tahan cuaca (weather resistant)
- **Spot-less** - Anti noda (stain resistant)
- **Easywash** - Mudah dibersihkan (easy to clean)
- **Vinilex** - Cat tembok standar
- **Komilex** - Ekonomis (economy)
- **Matex** - Serbaguna (multipurpose)

### Waterproofing
- **Elastex** - Cat pelapis anti bocor

### Cat Kayu & Besi (Wood & Metal Paint)
- **Bee Brand** - Cat enamel
- **Woodstain** - Cat kayu water-based
- **Bodelac** - Cat dasar + akhir (primer + finish)

### Cat Semprot (Spray Paint)
- **Pylox** - Cat semprot serbaguna

## Size/Unit Conventions
- L = Liter (for liquid paint)
- Kg = Kilogram (for heavy/thick paint)
- pcs = pieces (for spray cans, tools)
- galon = gallon (informal, ~3.7L)
